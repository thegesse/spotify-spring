## How to use
### find songs with the search bar then either play them or add them to a playlist, to create a playlist simply enter the name you would want to use in the side bar the nclick the + symbol next to it. To view what songs you added to a playlist you click on its name then a list will appear above the player with a play and a remove from playlist option. The video is required for pausing and playing the song, the 2 buttons at the buttom are used for shuffling and skipping a song.
